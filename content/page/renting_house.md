**目标:**    
- 找到并租到合适的房子（Find and rent a suitable house）  
  
**挑战:**    
1. 确定你的预算和需求，例如房屋大小、位置和设施    
   Determine your budget and requirements, such as house size, location, and amenities.    
2. 联系房东或中介，安排看房时间    
   Contact landlords or agents to schedule house viewings.    
3. 在看房时注意房屋的状况和周边环境    
   Pay attention to the condition of the house and the surrounding environment during the visit.    
4. 与房东协商租金、押金及租赁条款    
   Negotiate rent, deposit, and lease terms with the landlord.    
5. 仔细阅读租赁合同，并确保理解所有条款    
   Carefully read the rental agreement and ensure you understand all the terms.
